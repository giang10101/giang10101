Piotr Woś
Vice President

Piotr has been with eSky for seven years. His area of responsibility is adding new hotels to the eSky portfolio. Piotr has also led our IT project coordination team for two years. He began his career in e-commerce as a project manager for the online market of a large home appliances retailer.

Łukasz Neska
Vice President

Łukasz has been with eSky since 2008. His efforts have made the group's European services leaders in their countries, and the Brazilian service is in the country's top three. His career has included experience in Heritage Of Scotland.

Andrzej Kozłowski
Vice President

Andrzej joined the eSky group as its CFO in 2015. He has over a decade of experience in accounting, controlling, audit, and finances. His previous career experience has been with such companies as Mecalux, Deloitte, and Work Express.
